About Us

A comfortable homestay offering authentic Kerala hospitality

ABOUT US

Ella Homestay is a peaceful and welcoming retreat nestled in the heart of Kerala, offering guests a comfortable stay with warm, homely hospitality. Surrounded by natural beauty and a calm atmosphere, our homestay is ideal for travelers seeking relaxation, cultural experiences, and a true taste of Kerala living. We focus on cleanliness, comfort, and personalized care to ensure every guest feels at home. Whether you are visiting for leisure, family trips, or a quiet getaway, Ella Homestay provides a memorable and refreshing stay experience.


WHY GUEST CHOOSE SKYLINE HOTEL?

Our traditional Kerala cottage blends heritage craftsmanship with warm village hospitality. Built with timeless Kerala architecture, surrounded by nature and peaceful landscapes, our space offers an authentic experience of the state’s rich culture and serene way of life
Rooted in Kerala’s heritage, our cottage is a quiet escape designed with tradition, comfort and natural beauty
