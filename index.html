About Us

Rooted in the timeless charm of Kerala’s traditions, our cottage was created as a peaceful escape where nature, culture and warm hospitality come together. Inspired by the classic nalukettu architecture and the villages that shaped Kerala’s soul, we offer guests an experience that feels both ancient and beautifully alive. Here, the fragrance of coconut wood, the glow of old-style lamps, and the whisper of swaying palms create a serene retreat that carries the stories of generations.
